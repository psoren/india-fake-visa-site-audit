## Why this exists

Government services digitize at the speed of procurement, not at the speed of the web. The result is a layer of public-facing software that the average citizen has no choice but to use — to get a passport, a visa, a tax filing, a benefit — and that an outside engineer can usually reverse-engineer from the bundle in an afternoon.

This site is a catalog of those teardowns. Each entry looks at one government property, reads what's actually shipped to the browser, and writes down what's there. The tone aims to be precise and unsparing rather than mocking.
