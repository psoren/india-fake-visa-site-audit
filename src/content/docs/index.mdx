## Why this exists

The official Indian e-Visa portal lives at **`https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/`**. Everything else — `e-visaindia.com`, `evisaindia.org`, `visatoindia.org`, and roughly a dozen domains named in Government of India advisories — is either a third-party intermediary marking up a $25–$90 service to $150+, or an outright phishing operation.

This site catalogs those domains and reads their public-facing markup to document what they actually are: the hosting they run on, the trackers they embed, the brand-imitation language in the HTML, the disclaimers commented out in the source.
